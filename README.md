# Nike-Sales-Analysis-Dashboard-
👟 Nike Product Line – Sales & Performance Dashboard (Excel & Power BI)
<br>
📊 Project Overview
This Power BI dashboard analyzes the entire Nike product portfolio (not just one shoe). It provides a comprehensive view of sales, customer reviews, and regional performance across multiple Nike products.

📁 Data Source
Excel File containing:

Multiple product records (various Nike shoes/models)

Sales price & units sold per product

Customer ratings & review counts

Region-wise sales & operating profit

🧩 Dashboard Pages
1. 📄 Home / Overview Page
Product catalog view (multiple shoes)

Featured product: Nike Air Max 720 (as an example)

Brand description & tagline

2. 📈 Detail Page (Key Metrics)
Aggregated KPIs for all products:

Total Reviews: 80+ (across all products)

Average Rating: 9.10 / 10

Total Sale Price: $70K+

Total Units Sold: 20K+

3. 🌎 Regional Performance Page
Sum of Sale Price by Region – Map + Bar Chart

Operating Profit & Units Sold by Region – Dual-axis chart

Region breakdown shows sales contribution from all products combined

🛠 Tools Used
Power BI – Dashboard creation & visualization

Microsoft Excel – Data cleaning & preparation

📌 Key Business Insights
Northeast region generates highest overall sales (41.3%)

Average customer rating >9/10 – strong brand satisfaction

Southeast region has lowest sales – potential growth opportunity

Multiple products contribute to regional performance
